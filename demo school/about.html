Dr. Vijay Prashad

(principal)

Dr. Vijay Prashad is the Founder Director of Rising Sun High School and a dedicated educationist committed to shaping young minds through quality education and strong values. With years of experience in the field of education, he has worked tirelessly to create a learning environment that promotes academic excellence, discipline, and personal growth. His vision is to empower students with knowledge, confidence, and leadership skills, enabling them to succeed in an ever-changing world. Under his guidance, Rising Sun High School continues to strive for excellence and holistic development of every student.
